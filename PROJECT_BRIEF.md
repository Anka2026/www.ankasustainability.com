# ANKA Sustainability Web Project Brief

## Final Brand Direction

ANKA Sustainability is a premium multilingual corporate website for a sustainability consulting firm that also offers proprietary software solutions.

The website must present:
- Consulting services as the primary business identity
- Software solutions as a strong secondary capability
- A clean, premium, enterprise-level visual language
- A multilingual structure in Turkish, English, and Dutch

## Final Color Palette

- Primary: #2F3D4A
- Accent: #0891B2
- Background: #F7FAFC
- Surface: #FFFFFF
- Text: #0F172A
- Muted Text: #64748B
- Border: #D8E2EA

## Brand Feel

- Consulting-first
- Software-supported
- Premium
- Clean
- Corporate
- International
- European
- Trustworthy
- Structured
- Not playful
- Not startup-gimmicky
- Not template-cheap

## Core Design Rules

- The theme must remain stable from the beginning
- The site must feel premium and calm
- The design must support both consulting credibility and software product showcase
- White and light backgrounds should remain clean, not noisy
- Accent color should be used in a controlled way
- Typography and spacing must feel modern and high-end
- Avoid exaggerated animations and trendy gimmicks
- Keep layouts modular and reusable

## Website Goal

Build a multilingual corporate website that helps ANKA present:
- Sustainability consulting services
- Reporting and regulatory expertise
- Carbon, CBAM, LCA, EPD, and compliance services
- Proprietary software solutions
- A strong international image for Turkish, English, and Dutch audiences

## Target Audience

- Export-oriented manufacturers
- Corporate sustainability teams
- European-facing suppliers
- Industrial companies
- ESG / sustainability managers
- Decision makers looking for both advisory and digital tools

## Tech Stack

- Next.js with App Router
- TypeScript
- Tailwind CSS
- shadcn/ui
- next-intl
- Reusable component-based structure
- SEO-friendly page structure

## Main Navigation

- Home
- About
- Services
- Software Solutions
- Sectors
- Insights
- Contact

## Language Structure

Supported languages:
- Turkish (tr)
- English (en)
- Dutch (nl)

The website must be built with proper i18n structure from the beginning.

## Page Strategy

### Home
Main corporate landing page.
Consulting comes first, software comes second.

### About
Who ANKA is, its expertise, positioning, and working approach.

### Services
Detailed sustainability consulting services.

### Software Solutions
Product showcase page for proprietary software tools, demos, screens, features, and use cases.

### Sectors
Industries served and sector-specific expertise.

### Insights
Articles, updates, thought leadership, and regulatory content.

### Contact
Contact form, business inquiry structure, and meeting request CTA.

## Homepage Structure

- Hero Section
- Trust / Credibility Strip
- Core Services Section
- Software Solutions Preview
- Sectors Preview
- Why Work With Us
- Insights Preview
- Final CTA
- Footer

## Software Solutions Strategy

This section must not look like a blog list.
It should feel like a product showcase.

Include:
- Product cards
- Demo or request demo CTA
- Tech badges
- Screen / dashboard preview area
- Short use cases
- Clean SaaS-like modules inside a corporate site

Consulting authority must remain the dominant perception.
